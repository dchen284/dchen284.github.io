About Me

In past work, I enjoyed side projects to improve workflow, so everyone can leave work less stressed and more accomplished, with work being completed on schedule and to specification.

As a Software Engineer, using JavaScript, Python, React, Redux, Express, Sequelize, Flask-SQLAlchemy, and PostgreSQL, I hope to make my joy in "making work flow smoother" to be my main vocation, building new apps to unsnarl the tangles of work and life.

I am proud of my technical skill to research, experiment, and solve technical bugs, but even more proud to be able to mesh well with teammates, watching our pieces of work add up to more than the sum of its parts. My hobbies outside of work include piecing together strategies in board games and Magic: the Gathering, or piecing together small trips to visit new museums and restaurants.
